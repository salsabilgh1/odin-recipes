Kebab

Description

Kebab refers to skewered and grilled meat, common in Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cuisines. It can be made from lamb, beef, or chicken, often marinated in spices, yogurt, or herbs to enhance flavor. The meat is threaded onto skewers, sometimes with vegetables like onions and peppers, then grilled over an open flame or on a barbecue. Variations include shish kebab, doner kebab, and kofta kebab.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken or lamb, cubed
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt & pepper
  • Optional: bell peppers, onions (cut into chunks)

Steps

  1. Mix yogurt, lemon, spices, and garlic into a marinade.
  2. Marinate meat cubes for at least 1 hour (overnight is better).
  3. Thread meat and vegetables onto skewers.

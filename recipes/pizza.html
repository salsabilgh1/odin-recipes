Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a flatbread crust topped with tomato sauce, cheese (often mozzarella), and various toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, or vegetables. The dough is rolled out, topped with ingredients, and baked at a high temperature until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Regional variations exist, such as thin-crust Neapolitan or deep-dish Chicago-style pizza.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • Toppings (e.g. pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, yeast, and salt. Add water and olive oil. Knead into a smooth dough. Let rise 1 hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 220°C (430°F).
  3. Roll out dough on a floured surface, transfer to a baking tray.
  4. Spread tomato sauce, add cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

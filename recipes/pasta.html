Pasta

Description

Pasta is a staple of Italian cuisine, made from a dough of wheat flour and water or eggs, shaped into various forms such as spaghetti, penne, or fettuccine. It’s typically boiled in salted water until tender and then combined with sauces like marinara, Alfredo, or pesto. Pasta dishes can be simple, like spaghetti with tomato sauce, or elaborate with meatballs, vegetables, or seafood.

Ingredients:

  • 200g dry pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
  • for boiling water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • garlic cloves (minced)
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano or basil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente (follow package instructions).
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil, then sauté garlic until golden.
  3. Add tomatoes, herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Drain pasta and mix with the sauce.
  5. Serve with grated Parmesan.

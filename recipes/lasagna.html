Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked Italian pasta dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich meat sauce (often Bolognese), creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and grated cheese like Parmesan. The layers are assembled in a baking dish and cooked until bubbling and golden on top. It's hearty and comforting, ideal for family meals or special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • ground beef or minced vegetables
  • onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • cup ricotta or béchamel sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • oil, salt, pepper, basil

Steps

  1. Cook beef/onion in olive oil until browned. Add tomato sauce, salt, and basil. Simmer 10 mins.
  2. lasagna sheets or use oven-ready ones.
  3. a baking dish, layer: sauce, lasagna sheets, ricotta/béchamel, mozzarella. Repeat layers
  4. top with Parmesan.
  5. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden
